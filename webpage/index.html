Overview

Our project explores cloth simulation by modeling how fabric behaves when interacting with objects. The goal is to simulate a “virtual saran wrap” that realistically drapes over 3D surfaces.

Accomplishments So Far

We have implemented the core cloth physics system and successfully simulated cloth draping over a sphere. This establishes a working foundation for further visual and physical improvements.

  • Implemented cloth physics simulation
  • Achieved stable cloth behavior over a sphere
  • Verified deformation and interaction pipeline
